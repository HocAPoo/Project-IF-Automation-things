About Us

We believe hydroponics is the most cost-efficient and sustainable way to grow crops. In 400 square feet, we can produce 256 Lettuce heads at our maximum capacity. We have 32 vertical hydroponic towers which we use with LED lights to produce a variety of crops - from leafy greens to flowers to fresh produce. Our production cycle begins with a seed and ends with delicious and nutritious produce that we can share with our club members.

Mission Statement

To educate and provide research support for UW students and the local community in hopes to create a new generation of innovators ready to contribute to the growing field of sustainable farming.
